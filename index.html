About Me

I am 27 and currently live in Manchester. Alongside studying coding, I volunteer in a digital marketing capacity for a podcast about the UK Prison System Bird. In my spare time I enjoy hiking and I'm currently planning on a multi-day hike across Northern Spain.

My Background

After studying English at Lancaster University, I began copywriting for different companies, covering topics such as travel, music and restaurant reviews.

I then worked for a property developer as part of their in-house digital team for a number of years. Initially employed as a content writer, my role developed, and I became a Digital Marketing Executive. Here, I was responsible for maintaining multiple websites, online advertising and email marketing.

My Interest in Coding

As I had some understanding of the creation of websites, I decided to upskill and learn how to code, with the goal of becoming a Junior Web Developer.
I intend on utilising my digital marketing expertise, alongside the coding skills I have learnt to become more employable.

Why I joined Code Nation

After some research about Coding Bootcamps in Manchester, I came across Code Nation. Their 3 week course seemed an ideal opportunity to dip my toe in the water and test my aptitude for coding.
